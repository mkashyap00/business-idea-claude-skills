### Value Innovation & The Four Actions Framework

To break out of a hyper-competitive "Red Ocean" and create uncontested market space, you must stop benchmarking against the competition. True value innovation decouples cost from value.

Apply the **Four Actions Framework** to the industry's standard operating model:

1. **Eliminate:** Which factors that the industry has long competed on should be eliminated entirely? (These are often legacy features that no longer add value but consume resources).
2. **Reduce:** Which factors should be reduced well below the industry's standard? (Identify areas where products are over-designed to match the competition, driving up costs for no reason).
3. **Raise:** Which factors should be raised well above the industry's standard? (Uncover compromises that the industry forces customers to make).
4. **Create:** Which factors should be created that the industry has never offered? (Discover entirely new sources of value that appeal to non-customers).

### Targeting Non-Customers
Do not focus on how to capture more of the existing market. Instead, focus on the three tiers of non-customers:
*   **Tier 1 (Soon-to-be):** Buyers who minimally use the current market offerings out of necessity but are actively searching for a better alternative.
*   **Tier 2 (Refusing):** People who have considered the industry's offerings but consciously rejected them because they are too expensive or complex.
*   **Tier 3 (Unexplored):** People who have never considered the market's offerings as an option.
